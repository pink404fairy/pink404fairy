About Me

Hello, I'm Mary Ruoro, a 28-year-old entrepreneur and the CEO/Founder of an EdTech company in Kenya. Our company has been at the forefront of digitizing school systems, addressing the critical need for modern educational infrastructure.

Our cloud-based system is designed to meet the unique needs of any learning institution, enabling effective teaching and learning while freeing up valuable time for educators to focus on what matters most - helping students reach their maximum potential.

By providing teachers, administrators, and parents with powerful tools and insights to monitor student progress, we're creating an environment where every child can thrive in their educational journey.

The SAFER Approach to School Security

Sense

Advanced surveillance systems and sensors that provide real-time monitoring of school premises, detecting potential security threats before they escalate.

  • High-resolution CCTV cameras
  • Motion sensors and alarms
  • Environmental monitoring
  • Perimeter security systems

Alert

Instant notification systems that ensure rapid response to any security incident, keeping staff and authorities informed in real-time.

  • Mobile app notifications
  • Emergency alert systems
  • Staff communication channels
  • Automated incident reporting

Fortify

Comprehensive security infrastructure that strengthens school premises against potential threats.

  • Access control systems
  • Secure entry points
  • Emergency lockdown protocols
  • Visitor management systems

Engage

Community involvement and training programs that create a culture of security awareness.

  • Staff security training
  • Student safety programs
  • Parent security workshops
  • Emergency response drills

Respond

Efficient emergency response protocols and coordination with local authorities.

  • Emergency response teams
  • Police coordination systems
  • Medical response protocols
  • Post-incident support
